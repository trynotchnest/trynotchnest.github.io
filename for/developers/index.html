FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does NotchNest have a clipboard manager?
Yes — a full clipboard history with search, pinning and on-device AI rephrase, accessible from the notch.
Is it resource-heavy?
No. It's native SwiftUI, optimised for Apple Silicon, and sleeps when the notch panel is closed — single-digit MB of RAM.
Is it sandboxed?
Yes. NotchNest ships through the Mac App Store, so it's notarized, signed and sandboxed by Apple.

Make your notch work for you.

Native SwiftUI, Apple-silicon optimised, sandboxed via the Mac App Store. Sleeps when idle — no CPU drain.
