FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is NotchNest free for students?
Yes, NotchNest is completely free on the Mac App Store — no student plan needed, no subscription.
Does it have a study timer?
Yes. The built-in Pomodoro timer runs focus sprints with breaks and a daily streak, right in the notch.
Will it work on my MacBook Air?
Yes — on MacBook Air M2 and later it uses the notch; on the Air M1 it runs as a slim top-bar panel.

Make your notch work for you.

Free on the Mac App Store. Works on every MacBook with a notch — and on older Macs as a top-bar panel.
